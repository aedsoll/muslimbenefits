the team

those who hold this space are held to the standard of the prophetic example — knowledge, good character, and genuine care for the community. roles may overlap and will be updated as the team grows, ان شاء الله.

role one
إفتاء
iftaa — religious rulings and knowledge
religious knowledge
for those who are equipped to respond to questions about the deen — rulings, evidences, clarifications on matters of aqeedah and fiqh. those holding this role are expected to speak with knowledge, refer to the people of knowledge where necessary, and never speak without a basis.
current — iftaa
open — applications welcome
role two
أنس
uns — warmth, companionship, comfort
emotional support
for those who are able to hold space for community members going through difficulty — with patience, empathy and wisdom. uns is about being present, not just giving answers. those in this role listen first and respond with care, always pointing people toward الله and the people around them.
current — uns

open — applications welcome
role three
رَوْح
rawh — relief, ease, practical guidance
practical advice
for those who can help community members navigate real life — work, relationships, decisions, finances, personal situations. rawh is about bringing relief through grounded, sensible guidance that is rooted in the deen but meets people where they actually are.
current — rawh

open — applications welcome
﹡

roles are not rigid

these three roles will naturally overlap — a conversation about a practical situation often needs emotional care, and emotional support often calls for knowledge. moderators may hold more than one role, and assignments will be updated as the team grows and we understand people's strengths better. what matters most is good character and sincerity in serving the community.

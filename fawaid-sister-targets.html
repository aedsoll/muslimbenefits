your targets

these are the expectations held for sister mods. the aim is for every sister in this space to feel held, heard, and guided. these targets help make that real, ان شاء الله.

core responsibilities
١
target one
daily presence, with flexibility
the ideal is to check in daily — this keeps the sisters' section active and means no question or concern sits unanswered for long. that said, life varies, and consistency matters more than perfection.
check in daily where possible — even a brief scan of the sisters-only section counts
if you know you'll be away, please let the team know so cover can be arranged
prolonged absence without notice may result in a check-in from the mod team
٢
target two
build and hold the sisters' space
the sisters-only section should feel like a space sisters actually want to use — warm, purposeful, and distinct from the general feed. sister mods are responsible for making that section feel alive.
make regular posts in the sisters-only section — reminders, beneficial content, check-ins, questions for reflection
respond to sisters who post there — no question should go ignored
be a warm, consistent presence so sisters feel they can actually come to the space with real things
٣
target three
contribute content and knowledge
sister mods are not just moderators — they are contributors. the goal is a sisters' section rich with beneficial content, not just a space for queries and replies.
share reminders, athkar, beneficial quotes, or reflections relevant to sisters
you may also contribute to the general feed — posts that benefit the broader community are welcome
speak only with knowledge — if unsure, refer to the iftaa role or the general mod team rather than guessing
٤
target four
guard the boundaries of the space
rule 8 governs how brothers and sisters interact in this community. sister mods are the first line of enforcement for the sisters' section — this is not about policing, it is about protecting.
please remove any men's comments that appear in sisters-only posts without hesitation
flag or redirect any private DM requests between brothers and sisters — this applies to you too as a mod
uphold rule 8 consistently and without exception — the community trusts the space because you hold it
queries + discussions
↑
how to handle sisters' queries + discussions
follow this order — do not skip steps
١
direct sisters to the sisters-only section
if you see a general post by a sister that is better suited for sisters only, please remove it and dm her kindly to repost it there instead.
٢
respond if you are able
if the query is within your knowledge, answer it clearly and with evidence where needed. if it requires iftaa-level knowledge, say so.
٣
if unsatisfied — escalate via mod mail
if no answer satisfies the sister, please raise it with the general mod team through mod mail. keep it focused — help and the answer only, no unnecessary back-and-forth.
٤
or direct to a general post if it benefits brothers too
if the question is likely to benefit the broader community, encourage her to repost in the general feed — it becomes a فائدة for everyone.
♡

a note on flexibility

we are starting with four sister mods and building to eight, ان شاء الله. the team is small and real life varies — these targets are a direction, not a rigid checklist. what matters most is sincerity, consistency over time, and genuine care for the sisters in this space. communicate openly with the team if something becomes difficult.
